# OTC Drug Support Tool: Healthcare Project Management & Risk Planning

## Project Overview
This project focuses on the end-to-end **project management planning** for a proposed **OTC Drug Support Tool**, designed to help users make safer over-the-counter medication choices by identifying potential drug interactions and risks.

The project applies formal **healthcare project management principles**, covering scope definition, budgeting, scheduling, risk analysis, communication planning, and project closure documentation.

---

## Project Objectives
- Improve patient safety by reducing adverse OTC drug interactions
- Provide accessible, reliable information for informed self-medication
- Support safe OTC drug selection based on existing conditions or medications
- Deliver a feasible, maintainable healthcare decision-support tool

---

## Project Scope
### In Scope
- OTC drug information and interaction awareness
- Risk identification and mitigation planning
- Budget estimation and cost tracking
- Stakeholder communication planning
- User testing strategy and deployment planning

### Out of Scope
- Medical diagnosis
- EHR integration
- Prescription drug management
- Legal or regulatory decision-making

---

## Project Management Components

### Project Proposal
- Background, justification, objectives, scope, and timeline
- Hybrid methodology (Agile + Waterfall)

### Planning Artifacts
- **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)**
- **Gantt chart / work plan**
- **Communication management plan**
- **Risk register with mitigation strategies**
- **Detailed project budget and cost tracking**

### Risk Management
- Identified risks related to:
  - Data privacy and compliance
  - Security vulnerabilities
  - Data quality and integration
  - Cost overruns and testing delays
- Prioritized risks using Risk Priority Numbers (RPN)
- Defined mitigation strategies for each risk

### Budget & Cost Analysis
- Estimated vs. actual costs
- Labor, material, testing, deployment, and contingency tracking
- Cost variance analysis across project phases

---

## Methodology
- Hybrid project management approach (Agile + Waterfall)
- Structured planning with iterative feedback
- Stakeholder-driven communication and review cycles

---

## Lessons Learned
- Importance of clear scope definition
- Value of structured communication
- Necessity of early risk identification
- Effectiveness of WBS for task clarity
- Critical role of teamwork and stakeholder alignment in healthcare projects
